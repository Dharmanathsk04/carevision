Frequently Asked

Quick answers

Is this a medical diagnosis?

No. All tools here are for educational and awareness purposes only. For any medical concerns, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.

How accurate is BMI?

BMI is a screening guideline, not a direct measure of body fat or health. Factors such as muscle mass, age, and gender can affect its interpretation. Use it as one of several health indicators.

Is my appointment data safe?

Yes — all appointment data is stored in your browser's local storage only. Nothing is sent to any server. You can export or clear it at any time.
